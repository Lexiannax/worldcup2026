Road Closures

Permanent — all tournament dates: Marie P DeBartolo Way closed May 25 – July 3, 2026. East stadium access via Stars and Stripes Drive only.
On each match day — two phases:
Phase 1 — Tasman Dr from Great America Pkwy to Calle Del Sol.
Phase 2 — Extended closure to Lick Mill Blvd. Calle Del Sol closes to general traffic.
San Tomas Aquino Creek Trail also closed. Bike detour (~2.25 mi): Agnew Rd → Lakeshore Dr → Gianera St → Stars & Stripes Dr.
