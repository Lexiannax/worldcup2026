Match-Day Road Closures

Permanent — all tournament dates: Marie P DeBartolo Way is closed from May 25 through July 3, 2026. Access east of the stadium is via Stars and Stripes Drive only.
On each match day — two phases:

Phase 1 — Tasman Dr closed from Great America Pkwy to Calle Del Sol. Local detour: Great America Way → Great America Pkwy → Lafayette St → Calle De Luna/Calle Del Sol. Regional detour uses Hwy 101 and 237.

Phase 2 — Expanded closure to Lick Mill Blvd. Calle Del Sol closes to general traffic; Lick Mill Blvd becomes the alternative. Local resident and business access maintained.

San Tomas Aquino Creek Trail closed all 6 match days.
🚲 Bike detour (~2.25 mi): Agnew Rd → Lakeshore Dr → Gianera St → Stars & Stripes Dr.
🚶 Pedestrian detour (~1.5 mi): Mission College Blvd → Great America Pkwy.
