Matchday Transit Targets

Caltrain Connection → Exit Gate A
Exit via Gate A, board VTA Orange Line at Great America Station, ride to Mountain View, transfer to Caltrain.
BART Connection → Exit Gate F
Exit via Gate F, board VTA Orange Line at Lick Mill Station, ride to Milpitas, transfer to BART.
Accessible Golf Cart Shuttles
Continuous operation route run along Patrick Henry Dr directly up into the main entrance plazas.

Rideshare & Accessible Travel

North Pickup → Gates A, E, F
Red Lot 7 / Rideshare North. ~0.8 mi, 15–20 min walk: west on Tasman Dr, right on Great America Pkwy, right on Mountain View-Alviso Rd.
South Pickup → Gates B, C
Freedom Circle / Rideshare South. ~1 mi, 15–20 min walk: west through the stadium lots to Great America Pkwy, left (south), then left on Mission College Blvd.
Accessible Rideshare / ADA Drop-off
Mobility-needs guests: use Rideshare North (Red Lot 7), courtesy shuttle to Stadium Plaza drop-off. General accessible pickup/drop-off also on Patrick Henry Dr.
Private / Black Car
Curbside on northbound Lick Mill Blvd (east lane only, no double-parking or standing).

Red Lots

Red Lot 1 — Primary ADA
Closest accessible parking, entrance only via the Red Lot 1 gate. Includes bike valet and golf cart shuttle to the gates.
Red Lot 7 — Rideshare North + Overflow ADA
Doubles as the North rideshare pickup and the VTA ACCESS Paratransit pickup point. If Red Lot 1 fills, mobility-placard holders go here, plus Pink Lots 4/5 and Green Lot 8, all with courtesy shuttle.

Blue Lot 1

Premium / Tailgating Lot
Blue Lot 1 (with Green Lot 1) uses Levi's Stadium's prepaid "EET" entry system, a pass for this lot specifically is required. Tailgating is permitted here. Some third-party guides report ADA spaces and shuttle service in Blue Lot 1, in addition to Red Lot 1.

*Marked on the bowl diagram west of the stadium near the other close-in lots. The official 2026 lot color map wasn't in the documents available, so treat this position as approximate until confirmed on-site or in the FIFA app's stadium 3D map.
