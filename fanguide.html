Getting to Levi's Stadium
Levi's Stadium is at 4900 Marie P DeBartolo Way, Santa Clara, CA 95054. It sits between Great America Parkway and Tasman Drive, right next to California's Great America theme park. Transit is the recommended option — parking is expensive, slow to exit, and lots fill fast. Plan your route before match day and have a backup.
🚆
Caltrain + VTA Light Rail
Recommended from SF & Peninsula
1
Board Caltrain at any SF, Peninsula, or South Bay station toward Mountain View. From SF/4th & King add ~55 min
2
At Mountain View Caltrain, cross the platform or walk 2 minutes to the VTA Mountain View Transit Center.
3
Take the VTA 902 Blue Line toward Alum Rock. Ride 4 stops to Great America Station. ~12 min
4
Walk 8 minutes north from Great America Station to the stadium entrance. Follow the crowd or signs to Gate A (south) or Gate C (east).
Add 30 min buffer from SF for crowds. Buy a Clipper Card or use Apple/Google Pay on VTA. Single VTA ride is $2.50.
Total from SF: ~75 min Caltrain schedules ↗
🚇
BART + VTA Light Rail
Best from East Bay & Oakland
1
Take BART to Berryessa/North San José Station (end of the Orange Line from Oakland/Fremont). ~45 min from Oakland
2
Transfer to VTA 902 Blue Line at Berryessa toward Mountain View. Ride to Great America Station. ~25 min
3
Walk 8 minutes north from Great America Station to the stadium. Follow signs to your gate.
BART does not go directly to Santa Clara. The Berryessa transfer is the fastest connection from the East Bay.
Total from Oakland: ~75 min BART trip planner ↗
🚗
Uber / Lyft
Rideshare drop-off & pickup
1
Drop-off: Set your destination to Levi's Stadium — Gate A or Great America Parkway & Tasman Drive. Drivers use the north side approach.
2
Expect surge pricing on match day, especially in the 90 minutes before kickoff. Book early or use transit instead if you are price-sensitive.
3
After the match: Rideshare pickup is at Gate F / Tasman Drive lot (northwest side of stadium). Walk past Gate F and look for the designated app pickup zone. Expect a 20 to 40 minute wait after full-time.
Do NOT try to get picked up at Gate A or Gate C after the match. Drivers cannot access those areas post-game.
Pickup: Gate F / Tasman Lot Budget $40–$80 from SF
🅿️
Driving & Parking
Last resort — plan ahead
1
Take US-101 to Great America Parkway exit northbound, or Tasman Drive from Lawrence Expressway. GPS: 4900 Marie P DeBartolo Way, Santa Clara.
2
Lots open 3 hours before kickoff. Official lots are Gold, Red, and Blue. Budget $50 to $80 cash or card. Pre-purchase on SeatGeek or ParkWhiz to save time.
3
After the match, expect 45 to 90 minutes to clear the lots. Stay in your seat an extra 15 minutes if possible — the lots move faster once the rush passes.
Street parking in nearby neighborhoods is heavily restricted on match days. Do not risk a ticket or tow.
Arrive 2+ hours early Pre-buy parking ↗

From San José
VTA direct or short drive
1
By VTA: Take the 902 Blue Line from Downtown San José (Convention Center or Paseo de San Antonio stations) toward Mountain View. Ride to Great America Station. ~30 min
2
By car: Take US-101 North or Lawrence Expressway North to Great America Parkway. Distance from Downtown SJ is about 5 miles but traffic on match day can add 30 to 45 minutes.
3
If staying near SJC airport, the VTA 60 bus connects to the Blue Line at Alum Rock. Check VTA trip planner for your exact hotel location.
VTA is the easiest option from San José. No parking hassle and 30 minutes door to stadium.
Total by VTA: ~30 min VTA trip planner ↗

Stadium Gates — Where to Enter
Gate A
South side. Main entry from parking lots. Gets crowded. Walk from Great America Station heads here. Sections 101–115.
Gate C
East side. Good option if coming from Tasman Drive or rideshare drop-off on east side. Less crowded than Gate A.
Gate D
East side. Fastest entry line according to stadium staff. If your seat allows it, use this gate.
Gate F
Northwest / Tasman lot. Rideshare pickup point after the match. Also serves upper deck sections on the west side.
Check your ticket for your section number. Gates A and C handle the highest volume. Gate D moves fastest.

Match Day Practical Guide
🚪
Arriving & Gates
Arrive 90 minutes before kickoff minimum. For noon matches add extra time. Lines peak 60 to 75 minutes before kickoff. Gate D on the east side moves faster than Gate A. All entry requires a clear bag (12"x6"x12" max) or small clutch (4.5"x6.5").
☀️
Weather to Expect
Noon kickoffs: Open bowl, Silicon Valley sun. Bring sunscreen, a hat, water. West-side upper deck is fully exposed. Evening kickoffs: Starts warm, marine layer drops it to 55°F by halftime. Bring a layer.
🎒
What to Bring
Allowed: One clear bag, one small clutch, sealed water bottle up to 1 liter, sunscreen, medication in original packaging. Not allowed: Backpacks, cans, glass, selfie sticks, large umbrellas, laser pointers, noisemakers, outside food.
🍔
Food & Drink
Stadium food runs $18 to $25 per item. Concession lines peak at halftime. Buy before kickoff or wait until the 30-minute mark when lines thin. The fan zone near Great America Station has cheaper options if you arrive early.
📱
Tickets & Phone
Download your ticket before you arrive and screenshot it as a backup. Stadium Wi-Fi handles crowds but do not depend on it. Charge your phone fully. Concourse tunnels are signal dead zones. International visitors: check roaming settings before arriving.
